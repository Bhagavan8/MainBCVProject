Job Fair at Loyola Institute of Technology

Are you a fresher looking for job opportunities in Chennai? The upcoming Job Mela at Loyola Institute of Technology on May 10, 2025 is your golden chance to land your dream job with top companies. This mega recruitment drive will feature 50+ companies offering positions across various domains with salaries ranging from 20K to 60K per month.

Quick Job Fair Details

  • Date: Saturday, May 10, 2025
  • Time: 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM
  • Venue: Loyola Institute of Technology, Palanchur
  • Eligibility: Freshers (2025 batch) to 3 years experience

Job Opportunities

This job fair caters to candidates across various educational backgrounds and experience levels. Here's what you can expect:

Available Positions

  • Engineering: Software Developer, Mechanical Engineer, Electrical Engineer, Electronics Engineer, Civil Engineer
  • IT/Software: Java Developer, Python Developer, Full Stack Developer, QA Engineer
  • Management: HR Executive, Business Analyst, Marketing Executive
  • Other Fields: Pharmacy, Science, Finance, Customer Support

Salary Packages

The salary range varies based on your qualifications and the company:

  • Freshers (2025 Batch): ₹20,000 - ₹35,000 per month
  • 0-1 Year Experience: ₹25,000 - ₹40,000 per month
  • 1-3 Years Experience: ₹35,000 - ₹60,000 per month

Eligibility Criteria

The job fair is open to candidates from diverse educational backgrounds:

Educational Qualifications

  • BE/B.Tech in any specialization (2023/2024/2025 passouts)
  • Any Graduate (BA, BCom, BSc, BBA, etc.)
  • Post Graduates (MCA, MBA, MCom, MSc, etc.)
  • Diploma holders in Engineering/Pharmacy
  • ITI candidates for technical roles
  • Pharmacy and Science graduates

Other Requirements

  • Age limit: Maximum 31 years (for most positions)
  • Good communication skills preferred
  • Technical candidates should have basic knowledge in their domain
  • Must carry 8-10 copies of resume

Preparation Guide

To make the most of this job fair opportunity, follow these preparation tips:

Documents to Carry

  • 8-10 copies of your updated resume
  • Passport size photographs (5-10 copies)
  • All educational certificates (original + 2 sets of photocopies)
  • Government ID proof (Aadhar, PAN, etc.)
  • Experience certificates (if any)

Dress Code

First impressions matter! Dress professionally:

  • Men: Formal shirt with trousers, optional blazer
  • Women: Formal shirt with trousers/skirt or business suit
  • Avoid jeans, t-shirts, or casual footwear

Interview Preparation

  • Research about participating companies
  • Prepare answers for common interview questions
  • Brush up technical knowledge in your domain
  • Practice your elevator pitch (30-second self-introduction)
