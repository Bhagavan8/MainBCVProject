Job Description

As a Trainee Engineer at NNE, you will undergo a comprehensive six-month apprenticeship program designed to build strong foundations in pharmaceutical engineering. You will participate in structured technical training sessions, gain exposure to pharmaceutical manufacturing and compliance processes, and learn end-to-end project execution methodologies.

The role offers a balanced mix of classroom learning, hands-on experience, pilot project execution, and regular mentorship interactions to support your technical and professional growth. You will work in an engaging and collaborative environment that encourages innovation, curiosity, and continuous learning while contributing to projects that shape sustainable and intelligent pharma facilities for global impact.

Required Skills

Pharmaceutical Engineering Basics GMP Compliance Knowledge Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Processes Engineering Design Fundamentals Project Execution Understanding Problem Solving Skills Analytical Thinking Technical Documentation Team Collaboration Communication Skills Adaptability Continuous Learning Mindset Innovation Thinking Time Management Attention To Detail

Desired Qualifications

  • Education: Graduate or postgraduate degree in Engineering.
  • Disciplines: Biotechnology, Pharmaceutical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical or Mechatronics Engineering, Electrical, Electronics or Instrumentation Engineering, Computer Science, or Robotics and Automation.
  • Experience: Freshers or candidates with a maximum of six months of professional experience.
  • Language: Must be able to speak and write English effectively.
  • Attributes: Curiosity, proactive attitude, respect for diverse cultures, and ability to thrive in a dynamic environment.
