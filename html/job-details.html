Clear Your Browser Cache

Why Clear Your Browser Cache?
  • See the latest job postings immediately - cached versions might show old listings
  • Fix website display issues - outdated cache can cause layout problems
  • Ensure you're using the latest features - new functionality might not work with old cache
  • Improve website performance - remove outdated files that may slow down loading
Chrome
Firefox
Safari
Edge
Mobile
1
Open Chrome Settings
Click the three dots in the top-right corner and select "Settings".
2
Navigate to Privacy & Security
In the left sidebar, click on "Privacy and security".
3
Clear Browsing Data
Click on "Clear browsing data". Select "All time" as the time range and check "Cached images and files". Then click "Clear data".
Quick Tip: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + Delete (Mac) to quickly open the Clear Browsing Data window.
1
Open Firefox Settings
Click the three horizontal lines in the top-right corner and select "Settings".
2
Go to Privacy & Security
In the left sidebar, select "Privacy & Security".
3
Clear Data
Scroll down to "Cookies and Site Data" and click "Clear Data". Check "Cached Web Content" and click "Clear".
Quick Tip: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + Delete (Mac) to quickly open the Clear Recent History window.
1
Open Safari Preferences
Click "Safari" in the top menu bar and select "Preferences".
2
Go to Privacy Tab
Click on the "Privacy" tab in the Preferences window.
3
Manage Website Data
Click "Manage Website Data" and then "Remove All" to clear the cache. You can also search for "bcvworld" and remove only our site's data.
Quick Tip: Press Cmd + Option + E to quickly empty the cache in Safari (requires enabling the Develop menu).
1
Open Edge Settings
Click the three dots in the top-right corner and select "Settings".
2
Go to Privacy, Search, and Services
In the left sidebar, select "Privacy, search, and services".
3
Clear Browsing Data
Under "Clear browsing data", click "Choose what to clear". Select "Cached images and files" and click "Clear now".
Quick Tip: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete.
1
Open Browser Settings
Tap the menu button (three dots or lines) in your mobile browser and select "Settings".
2
Find Privacy Section
Look for "Privacy", "Security", or "History" in the settings menu.
3
Clear Cache
Tap "Clear browsing data", "Clear cache", or similar option. Make sure only "Cache" or "Cached images and files" is selected, then confirm.
Specific Instructions:
Chrome Mobile: Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data
Safari Mobile: Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data
Firefox Mobile: Settings > Delete browsing data > Cached images and files
